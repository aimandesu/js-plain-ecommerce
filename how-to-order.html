How To Order

Find A Product

Go to the Product page and go to shop session. There you could search the products that you want to buy. Click on the product that you want to buy to view all the corresponding products within it.

Note Product

In the list of all products that you choose to view, take a quick note of the products that you want to buy. All the noted products will be available at order page.

Fill In Form

Go to order page, then fill in the form from products that you noted to buy and send us the form. We will reach you immediately and enquire your purchases.

Payment and Product Delivery

We will issue the payment method that we accept and after payment have been made we will deliver the product to you.
