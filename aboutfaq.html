Our Story

KIMOYA CARD is a sole proprietorship that was founded on June 30, 2004. Ms. Tan Hwe Chin founded the limited company. Kimoya Card is a subsidiary of one of Kimoya Corporation's branches. Ms Tan's idea gave birth to the kimoya Card, which offers a variety of services such as printing, packaging, stationery and more.
